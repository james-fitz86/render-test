Lemon Drizzle Cake

Ingredients

  • 225g plain flour
  • 225g sugar
  • 225g butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 10g baking powder
  • Zest of 2 lemons
  • 50ml lemon juice
  • 50g icing sugar
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a loaf tin.
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then add eggs one at a time.
  3. Fold in flour, baking powder, and lemon zest.
  4. Bake for 40 minutes.
  5. Mix lemon juice with icing sugar.
  6. Drizzle over the cake while warm.

Carrot Cake

Ingredients

  • 250g plain flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 200ml vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 5g cinnamon
  • 5g baking soda
  • 200g grated carrots

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a cake tin.
  2. Whisk sugar, oil, and eggs together.
  3. Mix in flour, cinnamon, and baking soda.
  4. Stir in grated carrots.
  5. Pour into the tin and bake for 40 minutes.

Apple Crumble

Ingredients

  • 4 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 200g plain flour
  • 100g butter
  • 100g sugar
  • 5g cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Place apples in a baking dish.
  3. Mix flour, butter, and sugar to make crumbs.
  4. Sprinkle over apples.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.

Shortbread Cookies

Ingredients

  • 300g plain flour
  • 200g butter
  • 100g sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C.
  2. Mix flour, butter, and sugar to form dough.
  3. Roll out and cut into shapes.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes.

Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 200g butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 10g baking powder
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 50ml milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and line a muffin tray.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. Sift in flour and baking powder.
  5. Mix in vanilla and milk.
  6. Spoon into cases and bake for 20 minutes.

Vanilla Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 200g butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 10g baking powder
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 50ml milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a cake tin.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well.
  4. Sift in flour and baking powder, mix gently.
  5. Stir in vanilla and milk until smooth.
  6. Pour into the tin and bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Cool before serving.
